The BMW E21 320i

With 0 to 100 km/h (62mph) in 9.9 seconds, a maximum top speed of 113 mph (182 km/h), a curb weight of 2403 lbs (1090 kgs), the E21 3 Series 320i has a naturally-aspirated Inline 4 cylinder engine, Petrol motor, with the engine code M10/M64.
